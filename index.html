About PAR

PAN AM RAILWAYS(PAR) started in 1981, as Guilford Transportation Industries(GTI). GTI purchased PAN AM AIRWAYS in 1998. In 2006, the railroad was rebranded as PAN AM RAILWAYS. PAR is the largest regional railroad in North America. PAR has the rights and partnerships to carry freight from New England to New Brunswick to New York's Capital District. PAR has rights to approimately 1700 miles of track.

Why PAR?

Your business can reduce transportation costs by using the railroad. Our System has 4 Class I connections, and connections to nearly all New England Short Line railroads. This makes your business able to connect to the rest of the nation by freight railroad.
